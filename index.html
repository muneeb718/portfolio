Gesture Control Car

A gesture-controlled RC car powered by two ESP32 microcontrollers communicating over WiFi. The transmitter ESP32 reads hand tilts via MPU6050 and sends directional commands wirelessly to the receiver ESP32, which controls 4 DC motors through an L298N motor driver — all powered by a Li-ion battery pack.
